# Monetization

Primary monetization:

1. Affiliate links in review and comparison pages
2. Ebook sales
3. Email capture for nurture and follow up

Monetization rules:

1. High intent pages should have clear CTA placement
2. Disclosures must be present
3. Reviews should feel trustworthy, not spammy
4. Do not overload pages with ads
5. Ebooks should be tied to related content topics
