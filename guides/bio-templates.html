Guide

Bio templates for men

A good bio should be short, readable, and easy to reply to. Pick one angle, make it specific, and stop trying to explain your whole personality in 300 characters.

Quick answer

Use 12 to 35 words, one clear angle, and one reply hook.

The best bios feel like a real person wrote them fast. They do not read like a resume, a stand-up set, or a list of demands.

Witty minimalist

Cold brew, clean sneakers, and low-drama weekends. You pick the playlist, I will pick the taco spot.

Ambitious but warm

Building a good life, lifting heavy things, and still texting my mom back. Coffee first, chemistry second.

Adventure angle

Weekend hikes, weekday sushi, and a running list of places worth revisiting. Tell me your best local spot.

Old-school romantic

Plans dates, opens doors, and prefers a real laugh over endless texting. Best first date: coffee and a walk.

What makes a bio work

  • It sounds like you, not like everyone else
  • It gives one easy conversation hook
  • It pairs well with your photos instead of repeating them
  • It stays positive and easy to scan on mobile

What to avoid

  • Long lists of requirements
  • Trying to be edgy for attention
  • Generic filler like travel, food, and laughs with no specifics
  • Overexplaining what kind of relationship you want in the bio itself
