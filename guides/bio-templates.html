Six feet of decent decisions. Cold brew beats hot takes. Friday is tacos. You pick the playlist, I pick the spot.

Adventurous gentleman

Weekend hikes, weekday sushi. Passport has room and so does my calendar. Trade your best trail for my best roll.

  • Pair adventure with cozy food to feel grounded.

Ambitious builder

Startups, squats, and Sunday texts to mom. Building a life that is strong and kind. First round is cappuccinos.

  • Mix drive with warmth. It reads confident not cold.
  • Avoid resume speak. Be human and specific.

Old school romantic

Holds doors. Plans dates. Sends the text you made it home message. Looking for laugh till we snort chemistry.

  • Show respectful confidence and one concrete behavior.
  • Skip flowery cliches and stay simple.

Creative soul

Guitar, gallery nights, and photos of friends that actually turn out. I bring the playlists, you bring your favorite museum.

  • Mention one real creative output.
  • Name a local gallery or museum for replies.

Fitness forward

Morning lifts, weekend five Ks, balanced with burgers. If you like walks that turn into ice cream detours, say hi.

  • Keep tone inviting not intense.
  • Skip macros and numbers.

Bookish and brainy

  • Invite a suggestion to start a chat.

Dog dad

  • One real park or patio helps replies.
