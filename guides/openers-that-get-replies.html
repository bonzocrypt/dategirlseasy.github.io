Openers that get replies

Pick one opener, paste it, then follow the matching reply path. Keep it fun and respectful.

Direct plan Dog line Music and dessert Dinner challenge Travel hook Coffee order Teach me your hobby Pancakes or waffles Story prompt Movie or cook night

Direct plan

Two options: coffee this week or taco taste test this weekend. Which sounds more fun?

Follow ups
  • If yes: Let us do it. Wed 6:30 or Sat 1 pm?
  • If maybe: Quick call Thu to swap neighborhood picks?

Dog line

Your dog looks like the CEO. What is her policy on park dates?

Follow ups
  • If yes: Approved. I will bring treats. Sat afternoon?
  • If slow: I am booking the board meeting for Sunday strolls.

Music and dessert

Pick the playlist and I will pick dessert. Deal?

Follow ups
  • If yes: Chocolate or fruit?
  • If maybe: Mini test. 2000s throwbacks or 90s R and B?

Dinner challenge

Best quick dinner in our city is blank. Change my mind.

Follow ups

  • If slow: I will bring a tie breaker dessert.

Travel hook

FAQ

When should I ask her out after she replies?

Within 5 to 15 messages if the vibe is good. Keep it simple and offer 2 options for time or activity.

What do I do if she says maybe?

Reply with 1 easy next step: suggest 2 specific times or a smaller plan like coffee. Do not debate or over explain.

What if she replies slowly?

Send 1 light follow up that matches the opener tone, then stop. If she is interested, she will re engage.

Should I switch to a phone call?

Default to text. Only suggest a call if she already seems comfortable with it or asks for it.
