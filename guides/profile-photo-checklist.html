Profile photo checklist for dating apps

Quick wins that work on every app. Keep it natural and PG 13.

What to shoot

  • Clear head and shoulders in natural light
  • One full length standing photo
  • One candid doing something you enjoy
  • One social photo with friends where you are centered

What to skip

  • Blurry selfies and heavy filters
  • Group photos only with no solo picture
  • Anything not PG 13

Simple shooting tips

  • Face a window or open shade for soft light
  • Keep backgrounds simple and clean
  • Stand tall, relax shoulders, slight smile
  • Wear solid colors that contrast your background
