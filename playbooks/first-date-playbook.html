First Date Playbook: Easy Plans That Feel Natural

Updated 2025-09-19 • 3 routes • Exact texts included

Three low pressure routes with exact texts to set it up. Pick 1 and go.

Route A: Coffee and walk

60 to 90 minutes

  1. Coffee at a bright cafe, 20 to 30 minutes
  2. Short walk to a park or bookstore, 20 to 30 minutes
  3. Exit or extend based on vibe
Text to set it up: Let’s keep it easy: cappuccinos at [cafe] then a quick walk? Wed 6:30 or Sat 1 pm?
Extend if it’s going well: There’s a bookstore around the corner. 15 minutes?

Route B: Dessert first

60 minutes

  1. Meet at a dessert spot, 30 minutes
  2. Short seated chat nearby, 30 minutes
Text to set it up: I’m pro dessert. Gelato at [place] then a quick lounge nearby? Thu 7 or Sun 6?
If she’s unsure: 15 minutes to taste test and bail with no hard feelings if it’s not a fit.

Route C: Casual night out

90 minutes

  1. Meet at a lively spot with easy seating, 45 minutes
  2. Walk to a second location, 45 minutes
Text to set it up: Pick the playlist, I’ll pick the spot. Fri 7 or Sat 6:30?
Polite exit if needed: I’ve got an early morning. Let’s call it here, but this was fun.
