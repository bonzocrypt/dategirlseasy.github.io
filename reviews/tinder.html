Tinder review

Who it works for, pros and cons, and quick tips to get results fast.

Who Tinder works for

If you want the largest pool and you do not mind heavy competition, Tinder is still strong for volume. It tends to work best in large cities, and for people in the 18 to 30 range.

Pros

  • Largest user base of any app
  • Easy interface
  • Fast replies if your photos stand out

Cons

  • High competition, you need strong photos
  • Can feel casual or flaky
  • Algorithm favors recent, active users

Quick tips

  • Use 3 to 4 crisp photos, first one with clear face and a smile
  • Add 1 lifestyle or travel photo
  • Short playful bio usually beats long text
